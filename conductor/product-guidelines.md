# Product Guidelines: Ladakh Moto Website

## Brand Tone & Voice
The voice of Ladakh Moto should be a blend of **Adventurous & Inspiring**, **Professional & Trustworthy**, and **Welcoming & Informative**.

-   **Adventurous & Inspiring:** Capture the thrill of the ride and the raw beauty of the Himalayas. Use language that excites the imagination.
-   **Professional & Trustworthy:** Assure clients of their safety through expert knowledge, well-maintained equipment, and organized logistics.
-   **Welcoming & Informative:** Break down the barriers to entry. Make high-altitude travel accessible by providing clear, helpful information for everyone from seasoned riders to first-time family visitors.

## Prose Style
Content should vary strategically between sections but generally adhere to a mix of:

-   **Descriptive and Evocative:** For hero sections, blog intros, and destination highlights. Use sensory details to paint a picture of the landscape.
-   **Concise and Action-Oriented:** For itineraries, "What's Included" lists, and booking flows. Respect the user's time and need for clarity.
-   **Educational and Insightful:** throughout the site, but especially in travel tips and route descriptions. Position the brand as a local authority on safety, culture, and geography.

## Visual Identity
-   **Color Palette:** **Teal** is the primary brand color. It should be complemented by earthy tones (mountains, dirt) and vibrant accents (prayer flags, blue skies) to reflect the unique Ladakhi environment.
-   **Imagery:** High-quality, authentic photography of riders in action, serene landscapes, and cultural encounters.
-   **Typography:** Clean, modern sans-serif fonts for readability, perhaps paired with a rugged display font for headers to evoke an adventurous spirit.

## User Experience Principles
-   **Clarity above all:** Itineraries and pricing must be transparent. No hidden costs or confusing route details.
-   **Mobile-First Design:** Many travelers will be researching on the go. The site must be fully responsive and performant on mobile devices.
-   **Trust Signals:** Prominently display safety certifications, customer reviews, and contact information to build confidence.
